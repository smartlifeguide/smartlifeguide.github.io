## Understanding Brain Fog: More Than Just Being Tired

Before we explore the solutions, let's quickly understand what brain fog entails. It's not a medical condition itself but a symptom of various underlying issues, including:

*   **Stress and lack of sleep:** Chronic stress and poor sleep quality are huge contributors.
*   **Poor diet and hydration:** What you consume (or don't) directly impacts brain function.
*   **Environmental factors:** Poor air quality, excessive noise, or cluttered spaces can overwhelm the senses.
*   **Information overload:** Constant notifications and digital distractions can fragment attention.
*   **Lack of physical activity:** Exercise boosts blood flow to the brain, improving clarity.

While some of these require personal lifestyle adjustments, many can be significantly mitigated by smart home technology designed to create a more supportive and less demanding environment.
